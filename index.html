We Are

Abel Wong Gallery was founded in 2030 with a vision to become a pioneer in the digital creative industry. Starting as a small studio focused on multimedia content creation, the company was born out of the dream of Immanuel Abel as a founder, a creator with a deep passion for art, technology, and entertainment. Fueled by innovation, Abel Wong Gallery has grown into a company specializing in three core areas: video game development, professional video editing, and creative animation. Welcome to our apps!
